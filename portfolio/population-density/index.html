US Population Density

Authored by: Marcus Rodriguez

The map was made in QGIS 3.20.3 on Windows11 and used data from the US Department of Agriculture website as well as the US Census Bureau website. The data used to create the map include population densities measured in people per square miles in which I joined a csv file onto a US county layer in QGIS in order to obtain the measurements. Also, I used the coordinate reference system North America Equal Area (EPSG: 102008) for the county population maps and used the Coordinate Reference System WGS84 (EPSG: 4326) for my GeoJSONof Michigan with a precision of 4 that includes the state's population density in people per square miles and the county names.
